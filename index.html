My Process

Research

I explore different design trends, motivations, and ask people relevant questions about the product’s purpose.

Design

I whip up a few sketches, create wireframes and design mockups to establish organization and branding.

Develop

I implement the actual product through client-side framework (HTML, CSS, JS) or through graphical content or both.

Test

I assess through series of usability testing, gathering of critical feedbacks and decision on final product transformation.

Deploy

I examine user interaction through analytics after launching and conduct necessary maintenance.

Iterate

I repeat the whole process and apply new insights for better service on upcoming projects.
