What I've been up to (Projects)

Aldrich Tan | Product & UX Design

Aldrich is an Experience Architect and Design Consultant. He has worked with numerous companies in multiple industries throughout the US, Canada, and the Philippines. He specializes in building digital products from the ground up while weaving a brand's ethos into every fabric of the experience.

User Experience Society - DLSU Website

The official website of the User Experience Society - DLSU. The site features all important details of the organization, our Mission-Vision and other substantial content. Such is part of a larger initiative by UXSoc as a whole, that is, to unite all chapters into one domain with subsidiary sites for different channels.

UX Philippines 2019

UXPH is a non-profit organization that hosts the Philippines' largest community of individuals passionate about the realm of User Experience, and all things related. The organization runs annual conferences and monthly meet-ups to deliver valuable information, insight, and engagement to anyone seeking to learn and improve their craft.

DLSU COMELEC Automated Elections

A web-based online voting system designed to ease in the current voting process, promising convenience and simplicity for the voters and quicker computations of voter turnouts and canvassing for the COMELEC officers in an effort to be cost-effective and paperless.

DLSU Grade-Point Average Calculator

A responsive web application that computes for the student's term Grade Point Average (DLSU standard). It can determine a certain GPA whether it qualifies to the Dean's list or not. Accessible across any mobile device.
